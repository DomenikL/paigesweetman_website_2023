Welcome to the Paige Sweetman's

Collection

‘Spectre Protocol’ is a Character Action game which will be released on the PC and PlayStation platforms. The game will follow the main character Vax who works as a bounty hunter in the cyberpunk city of ‘Cyberia’.

Featured Artworks

As part of my school design project in which I tasked myself to design the world and characters for the fictional game 'Spectre Protocol' I also designed some game covers to go with them. These covers went through several iterations until I finally landed on my final design (voted on by my peers) which I then polished further and finished.

About Me

Hi, I’m Paige Sweetman, better known as Sappheiros online. I’ve been doing art for almost all of my life now and I’m currently working towards pursuing a career in the gaming industry possibly as a tech artist or a 3D modeler/texture artist. I’m in my 13th year at school (as of 2024) and I am hoping to study game development in 2025. For the majority of my artistic journey I have been entirely self-taught going to classes and short courses every few years to hone my skills further.
