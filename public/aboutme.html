About Me

Hi, I’m Paige Sweetman, better known as Sappheiros online. I’ve been doing art for almost all of my life now and I’m currently working towards pursuing a career in the gaming industry possibly as a tech artist or a 3D modeler/texture artist. I’m in my 12th year at school (as of 2023) hoping to graduate next year and study game development in 2025. For the majority of my artistic journey I have been entirely self-taught going to classes and short courses every few years to hone my skills further.
